3. You must be responsive to constituencies

4. Authority is largely derived from leadership of voluntary followers

5. A "money primary" sometimes intermediates before entering the real arena

6. There's disagreement about whether focusing on the opposition is a good idea

7. Grassroots adoption

8. Landslide victories are products of network effects

9. Picking winners is verifiably difficult
